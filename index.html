United States Disc Golf Championship

Rock Hill, South Carolina, United States

Directed by Jonathan Poole

  • Total purse (1999–2025): $1,480,241
  • Iterations counted: 26

Since its debut in 1999, the United States Disc Golf Championship (USDGC) has called the iconic Winthrop University Disc Golf Course in Rock Hill, South Carolina its home. Founded by visionaries Harold Duvall, Jonathan Poole, and Dave Dunipace, the USDGC was created to redefine elite disc golf competition — and it continues to set that standard today. Featuring a single-division championship format, a meticulously designed course, and a rigorous qualifying path, the USDGC stands as one of the sport’s most prestigious and influential events. Each October, the world’s best converge at Winthrop to compete for history on one of disc golf’s most celebrated stages
